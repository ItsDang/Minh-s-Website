Tragedy Strikes at Mount Rushmore. 'Lena Dunham faced certain death earlier this month when she visited South Dakota. Dunham, who rose to fame in early 2013 for her uncanny resemblance to childhood icon ";Little Critter" and a loaf of wonder bread, recalls the traumatizing ordeal. "I was minding my own business and enjoying the views of the PoC Hills when I saw a group of men desecrating the cliff face. I told them that what they're doing was culturally insensitive and they then resorted to threats if I didn't leave them alone. They called me sexist and derogatory terms when I confronted them." The men reportedly called Dunham "marshmallow" and "soggy bread". When asked how she felt about the harrowing encounter, Dunham stated that "I was shocked and taken back, this is the 21st Century, don't they know who I am? I couldn't believe they thought they were allowed to say those things to someone as famous and rich as I am, or I think they did." The four men are currently being held by authorities.
